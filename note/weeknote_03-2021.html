Weeknote 03-2021

It's been weeks since I last wrote the weekly notes. I'm not sure why I didn't write. I haven't been busy on Saturdays or Sundays, yet I didn't feel as writing.

One reason can be that I'm feeling overwhelmed with my thesis project. There's regular reading, writing and synthesising but I don't seem to make any progress.

Although I'm fond of the topic, I haven't gotten around to write about it as I planned. I feel insecure about my knowledge, so I'm hesitant to publish.

Yet, if I look at my past 2 weeks reading, then there's so many long and nuanced analysis on other topics. If I analyse myself retrospectively, then the consumption has increased over the few weeks. Still, I'm struggling to write or create anything related. So, it might be a sign of creative burnout.

Returning to the thesis project, we have a monthly checkpoint next Saturday. We should present our progress with insights. I'm counting the deadline to overcome the procrastination. I want to piece together a wholesome view of public libraries, their role in society (also how it has changed) and why they might be the best tool addressing the most significant challenges that wait ahead.

The following step is to start finding a specific problem to build a sample case and prove the value in real life.
